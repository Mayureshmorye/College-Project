Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is yoga?
    Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines that originated in ancient India.
  • How often should I practice yoga?
    For best results, it is recommended to practice yoga at least 3 times a week.
  • What should I wear for yoga practice?
    Wear comfortable, breathable clothing that allows full movement.
  • Is yoga suitable for beginners?
    Yes, yoga is suitable for all levels, including beginners. Start with basic poses and gradually progress.

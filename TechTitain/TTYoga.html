Frequently Asked Questions

What is Yoga?

Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines.

How often should I practice Yoga?

It's best to practice yoga regularly, but it depends on your schedule and fitness goals.

Can anyone practice Yoga?

Yes, yoga can be adapted for all ages and fitness levels.
